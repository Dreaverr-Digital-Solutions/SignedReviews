Connect Stripe, start collecting Purchase Verified reviews — at no cost.

Free during beta

One plan. Free during beta.

Signed Reviews is currently free for every business. We are running an open beta while we mature the product, talk to customers, and learn what works. There are no subscription fees, per-review fees, commissions, or platform fees.

What's included today
  • Stripe-native onboarding — connect your Stripe account in one click
  • Automatic Purchase Verified review invitations after each completed transaction
  • One-time, single-use review links tied to a specific Stripe transaction
  • Cryptographically signed (HMAC-SHA256), tamper-evident review records
  • Public, hosted review page for every business
  • Embeddable review widget for your own website
  • Public verification page for every individual review
  • Optional reviewer identity attachment via Google, GitHub, LinkedIn, Microsoft, Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter / X
  • Team seats and role-based access
  • Read-only Stripe access — we never charge, refund, or modify anything in your Stripe account

Future paid tiers

If and when paid plans are introduced, we will publish updated pricing and an updated Refund and Cancellation Policy first. We will give you at least 30 days' advance notice by email before any charges take effect, so you always have the option to discontinue use of the Service before billing begins.
