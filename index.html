How it works

Four simple steps to earning unbreakable trust.

1

Connect Stripe

Link your Stripe account in one click. We use read-only access — we can see transactions, never card details or personal data.

2

Invite Real Buyers

After each sale, your customer receives a secure, single-use review link tied to their specific transaction. No link, no review.

3

Reviews Get Signed

Each review is cryptographically signed and permanently linked to the real transaction. Optionally, the reviewer confirms their identity via Instagram, LinkedIn, or Google.
