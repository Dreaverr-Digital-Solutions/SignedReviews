The process

How it works

Four steps from sale to verified social proof — fully automated.

1 Connect

Connect Stripe

Link your Stripe account in one click. Read-only access — we read transaction details to verify purchases, never full card numbers or billing data.

2 Invite

Invite Real Buyers

After each sale your customer receives a secure, single-use review link tied to their specific transaction. No link, no review.

3 Signed

Reviews Get Signed

Each review is cryptographically signed and permanently linked to the real transaction. Identity confirmed via Instagram, LinkedIn, or Google.

4 Embed

Embed & Convert

Pull your verified reviews from the read-only public API and embed them your way — a wall, a carousel, a badge. Every visitor sees they're backed by real receipts.
